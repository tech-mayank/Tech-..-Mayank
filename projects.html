Mess Feedback Digital Register

This Application is based on Sentiment Analysis. Most of the times the mess secy does not find time to crosscheck every review provided by the students. So, to reduce that workload here is this application. This application is build on Django Web Framework. You just upload your review and it will classify it as good, funny or bad. All the analysis of the reviews will be shown to the admin of the portal and accordingly he/she will make the improvements.
