AI Based Covid-19 Solution Compliance

Covid-19 is one of the major pandemic that has hit the manking in its strongest form. And following the guidelines is the only vaccine. But I've realized that monitoring such guidelines manually is a tough task and therefore I have built an AI Based Product which serves as solution for Covid-19 Pandemic Situation. This is a robust AI based cross platform software which could withstand strong and weak light and could give accurate results even in extreme temperature and humidity. The software works in a number of steps. The first step is the screening for Temperature followed by Conjunctivitis(or any kind of redness in the eyes), the second is for mask detection and third checks for Surveillance for Maintaining Social Distancing.
