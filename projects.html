Attendance Management System using Face Recognition

It's a full fledge and complete solution for attendance managemnet not just for students but for an entire Institute. The attendance are recorded based on the facial features and get store in the databases based on the course, the particular student is attending. Not just students but the product also maintains the attendance of all the faculties and staff too. Basically the aim of this product was to use facial recognition as a medium of capturing the attendance and thereby making the systems contactless.
